 Little Joe's is a family restaurant and pub that was founded by our grandparents Connie and Joe Assenato Sr. in 1946. They had a vision of establishing a care free, friendly place where you can enter alone and walk out with a new friend. Connie and Joe Sr. sacrificed to make great food and drinks affordable to the masses so all can enjoy. Their son Joe Jr., the current owner, took to the bar with his passion of making drinks and his mother Connie tirelessly cooked her genuine Italian recipes. They have passed away but their creation of such a establishment lives on 67 years later with Joe Jr. and his nephews Michael and Peter.

    After a successful grand opening the business began to establish itself in the Taylor street community. Little Joe's has survived through the years because of the dedication from the family. The establishment got up on its feet with the help of considerate relatives such as my Aunt Deloris and Little Joe's brother Pete Sr. Little Joe's draws inspiration from all over the world and has always been welcoming to people from all different walks of life. Little Joe's most famous drink is "The Snakebite."  The inspiration from this drink came to Joe when he went on a trip to Las Vegas in the 1970's. He was amazed at the rattlesnakes in the desert and decided to capture some of that enthusiasm inside his passion of making drinks. The Snakebite has been Little Joe's favorite drink for over 40 years. Currently Joe Jr., or Little Joe, is the 2nd generation owner who continues to deliver a laid back atmosphere while maintaining an exciting venue to party with your closest friends and family.
